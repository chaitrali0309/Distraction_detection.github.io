About Focus Tracker

Focus Tracker is a browser extension designed to help you maintain productivity by blocking distracting websites during work hours. Whether you're a student, professional, or anyone looking to improve their digital wellness, our tool provides the structure you need to stay on track.

Built with privacy in mind, Focus Tracker processes all data locally on your device. We believe that your browsing habits are personal, and we're committed to keeping them that way.

Our mission is to help people develop better digital habits and achieve their goals by providing tools that promote focused, intentional internet usage.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Focus Tracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing information to external servers. All data is processed and stored locally on your device.

What We Don't Track

  • Your browsing history
  • Personal information
  • Website content
  • User behavior analytics

Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's local storage to save your preferences, blocked websites list, and usage statistics. This data never leaves your device.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the extension update notes.
